About Raytor

CRAFTING LIGHT
SINCE 1998

Raytor Industries Pvt. Ltd. is a leading Indian manufacturer of precision halogen lamps, serving OEMs, aftermarket distributors, and industrial clients across the globe. Our state-of-the-art facility in India combines advanced technology with decades of expertise.

  • Fully automated production lines with Japanese & German machinery
  • In-house R&D lab with life-testing and photometric measurement
  • Supplying to leading two-wheeler, four-wheeler & commercial vehicle OEMs
  • ISO 9001:2015 | BIS | ECE certified products
  • Export presence in 30+ countries across Asia, Africa, Middle East & Europe
